Frequently Asked Questions

My Communaty wants to cancel its subscription?
Communaty Administrators can change the organization's subscription level at any time, your billing plan will change in the next effective billing period.
How do refunds work?

Contact us and let us know what happened and if your claim meets our refund policy standards, we'll issue a refund immediately!

What are my payment options - credit card and/or invoicing?

You can use your credit card to pay for any plan. If you sign up for an Annual subscription we can send you a yearly invoice. Contact Us for more information.

Is there a minimum contract term?

No! You can start a subscription and cancel anytime.

We're adding new members - will I be charged?

No! Add as many members and guests as you like to your Communaty without paying anything extra.
