Member Reachability

Custom Topics

Targeted Audience

Engage Messenger

1. Define Member Reachability
A. Members control where they are reached
On Unaty, Members can add Contact Methods, or ways they would like to receive messages. They can add as many emails and phone numbers as they would like. Later, Unaty will also support Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, and more methods.
B. Customize when to be contacted there
A Member can choose for each contact method when they would like to be contacted. Based on a "Context" like Event Update or Survey, Members can toggle their Reachability for a Method.
C. Admins can see how to reach a Member
Admins can see how Members would like to be reached and can know right away the best way to reach all their Members.
Members can personalize through which channels and when they want to be contacted. Admins know how to best reach a Member.
2. Create Custom Topics
A. Members and Admins can add Topics
An Idea, Interest, or Goal brings a Community together. The groups of Members that form around common interests within the Community is what makes it strong. That's why Members can add topics on Unaty.
B. Members can choose to subscribe to Topics
Topics on Unaty may include free time activities, debate themes, or even sports teams. Members can subscribe to a topic easily to receive information about it and stay in the loop.
C. Admins can see their Members interests
Data is gold in the digital world, and with topics, Administrators can see exactly what their Members are interested in and can curate their events and content to better fit the wants and needs of their Members.
Members can subscribe to their interests and recieve information they actually want. Admins know what interests their members.
3. Build your Audience
A. Reusable, auto-updating Member Lists
Email distribution lists of yesteryear are very practical, yet as less people can be reached by email, they're losing their value. That's why we created Audiences on Unaty.
B. Add Members, Roles, Groups, Segments..
Audiences automatically generate lists of Members from all different data types within your Community. Choose from Single Members, Roles, Groups, Member Segments, and Topics.
C. Use and analyze your Audience
Your saved audiences can be used to send messages to, and you can even easily see the Reachability of your Audience - how many members will be notified, how many can't be reached, and so on.
Admins can easily generate lists of Members and these lists auto-update when a Members' information changes.
4. Send your Message
A. Choose the Content Type and Topics
Start by selecting one of the Contexts we saw earlier when customizing Member Reachability, and the Topics your Message relates to.
B. Enter your Message once
Enter a subject line and rich body text for your message and any attachments you might want to send. Unaty Messenger will automatically optimize all content for all different channels (email, sms, facebook..).
C. Send and let the Magic happen
Now, just hit send and watch as the Unaty Messenger generates a member list from your Audience, optimizes your content for different channels, and distributes your message to each member individually.
Admins can reach their Members more quickly and effectively with more relevant content.
