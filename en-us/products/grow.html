Grow Membership
Unaty Growth Service makes it simple to advertise and target potential members through Google Adwords. Create a tailored landing page for visitors, integrate Google Adwords, and start your first Ad Campaign in under 24 hours. Unaty Services are available to all Subscribers of Unaty Village or higher.
Recruitment Page
Raise awareness and collect emails through an easy to set up Unaty Recruitment Page. Get started and set yours up in under an hour.
Adwords Integration
Use online ads through Google and Facebook to reachnew Potential Members and gather their emails and phone numbers.
Simple Analytics
Live track the success of your campaign: see which emails/phone numbers have been collected, how many visitors you've had and who has visited.
Fixed, flat pricing
Unlike most group software, your costs won't go up just for growing your Community. Learn more about Pricing and earning .

Fun & Engaging for All
Unaty was built from the ground up to be fun to use. Members, Guests, and Admins can gain experience and level up for simply engaging with your Community. Their engagement will also earn Credits for your Communaty, which will let you can use to pay for Unaty Products/Services.
