    • Last updated: May 2nd, 2018
    It is your right as a user to request the full deletion of your personal data.
    If you wish for us to completely remove all personal information we have about you, please send us an email at datenschutz@unaty.de.
    To complete this action, you must provide us with the following information:
    • Your user email
    • A Proof of your Identity (Scan of Passport or ID-Card)
    We try to get better every day here at Unaty. If you'd like to have yourself removed, we would appreciate information about why you want to leave us very much!
