Our Mission
United in creating a better Future.
We believe that the world’s most pressing social problems are so ingrained and widespread that they cannot be addressed solely by governments and traditional social-sector organizations. The world is changing and so are the roles people, business, organizations, and governments have to play. We believe that together we can create a better world, and that by combining business principles with social objectives, „social businesses“ will have a much bigger role and responsibility in the years to come. Unlike traditional, donation-based corporate social responsibility activities, social businesses allow companies to directly use their skills, expertise, and business network to address a particular social problem.
By placing additional emphasis on social objectives and leaving a broader positive societal impact, companies can enjoy business success and maintain better long-term relationships across all of society—including public decision makers, opinion leaders, and influencers—through an integrated stakeholder-management approach is critical for building support for a company’s interests and operations.

Our Story.
We, Sean McCall, Tobias Wirtz, Fabian Ahrens and Ramin Rente, are members of various associations and organizations, from sports clubs to technical and hobby clubs. In 2014, we all became members of the same student association, where we have been very active for two years. We have discovered how important identity-building clubs are for the individual, how they can help in a new social environment and how they can intensify already existing friendships.
All of us were very surprised to not find one software that fits the organizational needs of engaged members and offers the other members easy participation. That is why we dedicate ourselves to the company Unaty Technologies full time and wholeheartedly.
