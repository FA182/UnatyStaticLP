What is a Communaty?
A Communaty is a private social network and administration system for Clubs, Organizations, and Small Businesses.

What counts as a Member?
A Member is one User that is part of a Commmunaty. A member can log into the Communaty and enjoy all the Features the Communaty has access to.

What is a Communaty?
An administrator is a member or officer that can has access to the admin area, and special management functionalities.

What is an Officer?
An officer is a role like president, treasurer, ect. that is always assigned one member to fill the role. An officer can be used as administrator.

What is the Member Growth Tool?
The Member Growth Tool consists of a dynamic landing page for your Communaty whose Data can be changed anytime to be used for marketing campaigns on Google, Facebook and more. With this tool you can see who clicks on your ads, and directly gather their contact information.

What is Association Member Sync?
On Unaty, Communatys can be linked to one another, which means complete Umbrella Organizations can be digitally connected to each other as they are in real life. This enables Member Data sync across Communatys, so that the member data that members, administrators across all Communatys enter flows into one Golden Source.
