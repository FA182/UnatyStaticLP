Why refer a community to Unaty?

Help transform your communities

We'll work with you to positively transform the commmunities you send our way. We go above and beyond to strengthen the social fabric of your community and help leaders save time on administration, reach more members, and increase participation.

We ❤️ our partners

We're dedicated to working with you every step of the way to Unaty implementation. Including raising awareness, holding educational webinars, providing brand assets and marketing materials, to demoing and getting the community started on Unaty. We'll be right there with you!

High earning potential

You’ll earn a 20% commission every month that your referred customer stays with us. That means if you send us three customers who pay $449/month, you’ll earn $269 every month!

The Fine Print

Credit where credit is due
In some cases, we can give credit to a referral partner even if the customer didn’t sign up through their link (for example, if they didn’t sign up through their link by mistake). If you have a case like that, contact us and we’ll do our best to help.
Self-referrals

Self-referrals are not allowed. The point is to get you to refer other people. This is not a way to get a discount on your own account.

Commission Approval

Referral commissions are approved on a monthly basis. There is a minimum delay of 30 days before approving pending commissions (to account for any refunds since we have a 30-day refund policy).

Payouts

Payouts are made 30 days after the referral was made.

Terms

We reserve the right to change the terms of the Refer-a-Community program at any time.
