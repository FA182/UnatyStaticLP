Custom Development Priority Program

Work with us to develop a feature or product your community needs, built on Unaty.
We'll help define a custom solution, then we split the development cost.

How does it work?

Define the Job-to-be-Done

Tell us about the problem you're trying to solve, how you're filling that gap today, and what you'd like to accomplish. We'll work with you to outline the feature and development work in a way that works for your community and gets to the root of the issues you want solved.

Technical & Priority Analysis

We'll take the outline back to our team and work out how the solution will be integrated with your Communaty so you can have more time for your organization. We'll send an overview of the dev costs, of which we'll cover 60% and you pay 40%.

We build your new Feature!

Now our dev team will build the feature, gathering your feedback along the way to make improvements. We won't stop development until you're 100% satisified!

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the development cost split?
We'll cover 60% of the cost, and your community will pay 40%. Cost varies depending on the scope of the feature. You pay half at the start of work, half when work has completed.
How do you choose which projects to take on?

We first seek to understand why communities are asking for certain features and will take into consideration if this is a relevant issue for other communites. We also consult our roadmap to see if certain items fit in our current timeframe.

How long does development take?

Depends on the feature scope, small improvements usually a week, medium features 3-4 weeks, and larger features anywhere from 1-4 months.

What if I'm looking to build an integration?

You're still in the right place, let us know what you'd like to integrate with!
