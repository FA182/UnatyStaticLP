Unaty Brand Guidelines

We written some guidelines to help you use our brand and assets, including our logo, content and trademarks without having to sign legal agreements to use these assets.
If you have any questions contact us anytime at feedback@unaty.de.

Usage

The Unaty marks include the Unaty name and logo, and any word, phrase, image, or other designation that identifies the source or origin of any of Unatys's products. Please don't modify the marks or use them in a confusing way, including suggesting sponsorship or endorsement by Unaty or in a way that confuses Unaty with another brand.

Unaty Logo

When using the Unaty hexagon logo with other logos and graphic elements, maintain a padded space that equals 200% the size of the square around the hexagon.

The colored version of the Unaty hash logo can ONLY be used on a white or light-grey background. If you are using the Unaty logo on any other background color, use either the dark grey or white monochrome logo.

Do not place the Unaty hexagon logo over background colors that clash. If in doubt, use monochrome!

Naming

You're entitled to say that your website or application is integrated with Unaty (we love Unaty Integrations!), but please don't use the Unaty marks or name as part of your company, application, product, or service, or in any logo you create.

General Info

Using the Unaty marks means you agree to follow these guidelines as well as our Terms of Service and all our policies. Unaty reserves the right to cancel, modify, or change the permission in these guidelines at any time at its sole discretion. For further information about use of the Unaty marks and name, please contact feedback@unaty.de.
