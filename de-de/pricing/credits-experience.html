On Unaty all actions you take on the Platform earn you experience which lets you gain levels, for some actions like giving product feedback you will also earn Credits that can be used to pay for Unaty Products and Services.

User Experience & Level
The more you interact with Unaty, the better you understand and know how to use it. Virtually every action on Unaty, inviting a member, creating an officer, etc. earns you Experience Points (XP). Getting enough experience lets you level up, showing your Communaty and the world that you are knowledgeable and put time and effort into helping your Communaty.
Action Reward Description
Product Feedback 3 XP for each feedback item submitted. 7 XP if Unaty starts working on it, 10 XP if we complete work. Extra 5 XP if an important feedback item, 10 XP if marked as a critical feedback item. For interacting on our Communaty Forum, commenting, posting you will earn experience.
Create Communaty 100 XP Each Communaty you create will earn you experience points.
Join Communaty 25 XP Each friend you invite to Unaty that signs up will earn you 50 experience points.
Complete Global Profile 30 XP Adding a profile picture and other information for your global profile profile will earn you points.
Invite Friends 50 XP for each Friend that signs up. Each friend you invite to Unaty that signs up will earn you 50 experience points.
Invite Communaty 500 XP for each Communaty that fully joins Unaty from your Invite. Inviting a new Communaty to Unaty will earn you experience as well.

Frequently Asked Questions
Questions from the Communaty about credits, experience, and levels. If you don't see an answer to our question here, don't hesitate to contact us.

What can I use credits for?
Credits can be used by Communatys to pay for Unaty Products and Services. They will be used automatically to pay for your Communatys Payment Plan.

Do I have the same Level in every Communaty?
Yes! Your experience and levels are tied to your Global User, which can be a member of multiple Communatys.

Do the Credits I earn go to my Communaty or my User?
The credits you earn within a Communaty for inviting members and completing other actions like giving product feedback earn Credits directly for your Communaty. Actions like inviting Communatys to Unaty earns Credits that go to your user, that you can use for the Commmunaty you choose.
